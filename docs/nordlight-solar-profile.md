NordLight Solar Solutions is a small but growing solar energy company based in Tallinn, Estonia. Founded in 2019 by Kristjan Tamm, a former electrical engineer, the company designs and installs photovoltaic systems for residential homes and small commercial properties across Estonia. Their mission is to make solar energy accessible and affordable for Baltic homeowners and businesses.

The company has grown from 3 to 15 full-time employees and expanded from Tallinn into Pärnu, Tartu, and smaller towns. They typically handle 10–15 active installation projects at any time, with a mix of small residential jobs and larger commercial contracts.
